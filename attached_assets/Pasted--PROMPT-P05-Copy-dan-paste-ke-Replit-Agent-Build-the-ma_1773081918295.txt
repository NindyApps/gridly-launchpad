  WHERE workspace_id = current AND dismissed = false
  ORDER BY confidence_score DESC, created_at DESC
  LIMIT 50
 
Apply filters from filter bar state.
Show skeleton loading cards (3 of them) while loading.
 
REALTIME UPDATES:
  Subscribe to Supabase Realtime on intent_signals table,
  filter: workspace_id = current workspace.
  When INSERT event received:
    - Increment newSignalsCount state
    - Show banner at top: '3 new signals — Click to refresh'
    - On banner click: reload feed, reset newSignalsCount
 
EMPTY STATES:
  No trackers: illustration + 'Create your first tracker' CTA → /trackers
  Has trackers, no signals: illustration + 'Signals appear within 15 minutes'

POST handler:
